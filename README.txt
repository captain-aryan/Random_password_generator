RANDOM PASSWORDS GENERATOR

USAGE:
-l [LOWERCASE], Enters the lowercase list
-u [UPPERCASE], Enters the uppercase list
-n [NUMBERS], Enters the numbers list
-s [SPECIAL_CHARACTERS], Enters the special characters list

Lenght of the password is must be in numeric format (0-infite)

EXAMPLE:
For Perfect Password:
    Your CMD > -luns
    Length of Password > 20
    Random Passowrd is: xP1ge!NDjz_;9.30;baD

For Lowercase:
    Your CMD > -l
    Length of Password > 10
    Random Passowrd is: vaxaoxxvyp

For Uppcase:
    Your CMD > -u
    Length of Password > 8
    Random Passowrd is: CMPMMNPJ

For Numbers:
    Your CMD > -n
    Length of Password > 12
    Random Passowrd is: 361806153324

For SPECIAL_CHARACTERS:
    Your CMD > -s
    Length of Password > 10
    Random Passowrd is: ~`>.#\=*"*
